What Bastion checks

The same surface an attacker sizes up before deciding whether your site is worth their time.

Transport security

HTTPS enforcement and automatic upgrades from insecure HTTP.

Security headers

HSTS, Content-Security-Policy, clickjacking and MIME-sniffing defenses.

Cookie hardening

Secure, HttpOnly and SameSite flags on every cookie your site sets.
